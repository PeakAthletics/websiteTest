Jacob Storch, BS, TPI

Director of Training

Jacob was born and raised in Potomac Maryland. He went to the University of Colorado at Boulder, where he graduated with a B.A. in Integrative Physiology in 2020. Jacob comes to PEAK after working for the Washington Nationals in their strength and conditioning department. At the professional level, Jacob aided in the sports performance, mental development, and nutrition of Major League Baseball players. As a Former Collegiate Golfer, Jacob has a very strong background in the game of golf. Jacob is an avid golfer and enjoys to golf competitively in his free time. In 2022 Jacob became a TPI Level 1 golf performance coach. Jacob is also proficient in K-motion and conducting 3D assessments. With Jacob's passion for rotational sports and sports performance, he always looks for novel solutions to enhance the performance of his players. Jacob has brought a new innovated and multifaceted approach to training multiple sports at peak. He has started a new baseball division, at PEAK, to enhance the performance of young baseball talent around the DMV.
