Technology and Data

At Peak Athletics, we use the cutting-edge technology and data to create a personalized improvement plan.

Peak Athletics not only has the technology and data, but also our instructors know how to interpret and implement it.
You can visit us to get information for your coach, or we can work directly with you to optimize your swing efficiency.

What We Use

Golf Biodynamics (GBD) Software

Photo of the data capture is from Golf Biodynamics

This consists of high speed electromagnetic sensors to track a golfer's body movements, specifically hips, torso, lead arm, and club head. We use it to diagnose where you are gaining/losing power, might be at risk for injury, reasons for common weaknesses in your golf game, and opportunities to improve swing efficiency.

K-Vest Biofeedback System

Photo of the data capture is from K-Vest

The K-Vest is a biofeedback technology consisting of two wireless sensors. The sensors are attached to our K-Motion system that measures a user's movement patterns to determine if their swing aligns with a prescribed range of motion personalized for their golf swing and body.

HackMotion Wrist Sensor

Photo of wrist sensor in action is from HackMotion website

The HackMotion wrist sensor captures a user's flexion and extension values at Address, Top, and Impact. Using recommended Tour Pro ranges as well as customizable ranges, HackMotion provides instant audio feedback on a user's wrist movement. HackMotion also provides personalized swing drills.

Uneekor Eye XO

Photo of the Uneekor simulator in our Peak Athletics Ashburn facility

At Peak Athletics Ashburn, we have 3 Uneekor Eye XO simulators where players can record their club and swing data. The simulators capture Distance (carry, total, side) Smash Factor, Club Path, Dynamic Loft, Attack Angle Speed (club and ball), Spin: (back and side), and Angle (launch, side, and descent).

Foresight GCHawk

Photo of Michael Brennan using the ForeSight simulator in our Peak Athletics Ashburn facility

At Peak Athletics Ashburn, we have a Players Lounge outfitted with two couches, a TV, kitchen area, and a ForeSight GCHawk simulator. The simulator captures club and ball data for a player's whole bag (including putter!). Data includes Ball Speed, Vertical and Horizontal Launch Angle, Total Spin & Spin Axis, Carry Distance, Club Head Speed, Smash Factor, Angle of Attack, Club Path, Loft at impact, Lie & Face Angle at impact, and Impact Location on club face. Player can also test their skills on 10 golf courses.
